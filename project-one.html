Abstract Shape Design

For this project, I designed my own abstract shape and used the shape to create different compositions. There are nine compositions below.

I choosed black, blue, and purple as the colors of the shape. For the last three compositions, I played with the transparency of the colors in order to create different effects.

  • Symmetry, same size, and black and white

  • Symmetry, same size, and color

  • Asymmetry, same size, and color

  • Asymmetry, different size, and color

  • Asymmetry, different size, color, and weight

  • Asymmetry, different size, color, layer

  • Asymmetry, different size, color, and transparency

  • Asymmetry, different size, color, and transparency

  • Asymmetry, different size, color, and transparency
